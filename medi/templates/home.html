About Our Pets Clinic

Welcome to petscorp.in, a place where pet lovers come together to share their passion for animals and connect with others who share their interests. Our platform is designed to provide a fun and interactive environment where you can find information about the nearest pet stores, vets, salons, boardings or any other service provider, share stories of your own furry friends, and connect with other pet parents nationwide. Whether you're a dog lover, a cat person, or have a fondness for more exotic creatures, our platform has something for everyone. So come join our community and let's celebrate the joy of pets together! We started this platform after seeing the serious issue of pet abandonment, which affects countless animals around the world. Every year, thousands of pets are left on the streets or surrendered to shelters, where they may face uncertain futures or even euthanasia.
